FAQ

Got questions? We've got answers.

Can I connect both Facebook and Instagram?

Yes. Connect your Facebook Pages and linked Instagram Business profiles in one workspace. Manage everything from a single dashboard.

Is publishing fully automatic?

Yes. Once scheduled, our workers automatically publish at the right time with smart retry logic. Failed posts auto-recover without manual intervention.

How does AI generate recommendations?

AI reads your stored insights, post performance, and cadence data, then generates profile-specific action plans, KPI targets, and content ideas based on real numbers.

Can I schedule videos and Reels?

Yes. Upload MP4/MOV videos and they'll be published as Instagram Reels or Facebook Videos automatically. Image posts are also optimized for each platform.

Is my data safe?

All Meta tokens are encrypted at rest using Fernet encryption. We follow security best practices including HTTPS, CSRF protection, and secure session handling.
