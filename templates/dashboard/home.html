Applied Scope OAuth + token operations

New values are used for Meta connect flow and token validation checks after save.

How To Get Values Find META_APP_ID, META_APP_SECRET, META_REDIRECT_URI
  1. Step 1: Go to developers.facebook.com/apps and click Create App.
  2. Step 2 (Use case): Select Business. This app works for Facebook Pages + Instagram Business.
  3. Step 3 (Customize use case): If Meta asks to customize, keep options related to Facebook Login, Pages access, Instagram publishing, and Insights.
  4. Step 4 (API setup with login): Add/enable Facebook Login. In its settings, add your callback URL in Valid OAuth Redirect URIs.
  5. Step 5 (Get App ID): Open App Settings -> Basic, copy App ID, paste into META_APP_ID.
  6. Step 6 (Get App Secret): In same screen click Show for App Secret, paste into META_APP_SECRET.
  7. Step 7 (Set Redirect URI): Use exact value like https://your-domain/auth/meta/callback for both Meta Login settings and META_REDIRECT_URI.
  8. Step 8 (FB + IG linking): Your Instagram must be Professional and linked to a Facebook Page. Page role should be admin/editor.
  9. Step 9 (Business Integration selection): During connect flow, select all required Facebook Pages and Instagram profiles.
  10. Step 10: Save here, then go to Accounts -> Connect Facebook + Instagram to verify.

Permissions/Scopes to add (required for this app)

  • pages_show_list
  • pages_read_engagement
  • pages_manage_posts
  • pages_read_user_content
  • read_insights
  • instagram_basic
  • instagram_content_publish
  • instagram_manage_insights
  • business_management (optional but useful in some business setups)

Note: some tutorials say instagram_content_publishing; this project uses instagram_content_publish.

After save, use Connect Facebook + Instagram on Accounts page to verify credentials and sync pages.
