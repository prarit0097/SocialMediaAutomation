Recommended Daily Rhythm

What most operators do in order.

The dashboard feels simpler when each workspace is used for one specific decision.

  • Accounts first whenever assets are reconnected or token health needs review.
  • Planning for calendar structure and Scheduler for actual publish jobs.
  • Insights after posts go live to understand reach, interactions, and post results.
  • AI Insights when you want next-step recommendations grounded in stored data instead of guesswork.
  • Subscription and Profile only when access, expiry, or operator identity needs attention.

Operational Reliability

Designed to feel calm during daily execution.

Postzyo keeps publishing, reporting, and AI centered around the same connected-account model so teams spend less time guessing which profile, token, or row is safe to use.

  • Token health and stale-row checks reduce avoidable Meta failures.
  • Daily heavy snapshots keep Insights and AI pages fast and consistent.
  • Queue-safe publishing and retry handling protect scheduled work when Meta is noisy.
  • Billing stays isolated in Subscription so operational pages remain focused.
